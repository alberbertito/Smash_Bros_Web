About Smash Bros. 4

Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS / for Wii U is the fourth edition of the Smash Bros. franchise, being the first edition playable in a portable console.

There are near 60 different characters, every one with their different techniques and strategies.

In this game every frame is important. The game works at 60 fps (frames per second), so a single frame may seem useless, but the fact that in this game the slowest attacks come out at frame 14-15, about a quarter of a second.

That's why it's worth mastering the bigger amount of advanced techniques possible. A difference of a frame may mean win or lose a stock. In a 2-stocks metagame this is very important.

It's impossible to cover all the particular techniques of each character, but in this webpage you have the most common techniques that every character can perform. Happy smashing!
