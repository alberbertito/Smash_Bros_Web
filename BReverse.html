B Reverse

B reverse is one of the most useful techniques in Smash Bros. history.

You only have to input the opposite direction you are facing just after or just before pressing the B button.

Jumping backwards Press B + opposite direction Attacking facing to the front

When you input the opposite direction you change your momentum in the air, and when you do it just after pressing B your momentum doesn't change. B reverse can be performed in the air or grounded, and with any special move: side B, up B, down B and neutral B.

RAR is specially useful when you want to input a back aerial move as soon as possible. You can combine it with B Reverses as well to mix up your approaches.
