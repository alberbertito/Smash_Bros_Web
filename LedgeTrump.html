Ledge Trump

As you may know, differently from other previous Smash Bros. games, now more than one fighter can grab the ledge at the same time, but only for 20 frames though.

Ledge trumping is a technique that consists in taking advantage of this game mechanic to put the opponent in a disadvantage situation.

To perform a ledge trump you have to run towards the ledge while your opponent is holding it. When you pass the ledge you have to flick the joystick down and backwards to grab the ledge. This will make your opponent lose the ledge invincbility, which allows you to attack freely.
