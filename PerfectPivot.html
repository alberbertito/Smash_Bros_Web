Perfect Pivot

The perfect pivot is one of the most useful movements in the game. It consists in flicking the joystick to the right or left and then flicking it in the opposite direction.

What makes specially difficult this tech is that you have to do that in only 2 frames, one for each direction.

Frame 0 Frame 1 Frame 2 Frame 3

This will help you to position yourself better than with dash dance, foxtrot or extended das dance because it's faster, and you can input an attack in the frame 3 after spacing the attack with the perfect pivot.
