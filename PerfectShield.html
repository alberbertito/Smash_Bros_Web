Perfect Shield


The perfect shield is performed by pressing the shield button within 3 frames before an attack hits your shield.

This allows you to get rid of the shield lag frames when any attack hit a shield, being able to release before your shield as well as attack or move sooner. Perfect shield is also inmune to shield breaks.
