Reverse Aerial Rush

The reverse aerial rush (RAR) is a movement tech that will allow you to increase your offensive pressure.

The RAR is performed by pointing the joystick in the opposite direction just before jumping while you are running.
Run Joystick in the opposite direction. Jump

RAR is specially useful when you want to input a back aerial move as soon as possible. You can combine it with B Reverses as well to strong>mix up youor approaches.
