Tech


Probably the most basic technique of all Smash Bros. games.

A tech is performed by pressing the shield button within 20 frames before hitting a wall or the floor while you are in hitstun. If you don't tech in time, you will bounce on the wall or on the floor, which makes you vulnerable to attacks.

You can also wall tech jump if you press up while teching.
